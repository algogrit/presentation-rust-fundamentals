## Running & Building

- `rustc`

  - For compiling, a `.rs` to a binary

- `cargo`

  - For managing, rust projects. eg: `cargo build`, `cargo run`

## Naming Conventions

---

- functions, variables (let), modules & filenames

  `snake_case`

- constants

  `SCREAMING_SNAKE_CASE`

- Enums, Structs, Traits

  `PascalCase` or `UpperCamelCase`
